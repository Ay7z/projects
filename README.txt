# IPL Cricket Analytics

This project analyzes IPL ball-by-ball delivery data from 2008 to 2020 
using Python and Pandas to uncover performance trends across batters and bowlers.

## Dataset
The dataset contains match level and ball by ball delivery data spanning 
13 IPL seasons. A sample of 50,000 deliveries was used for this analysis.

## Questions Answered
1. Who scored the most runs overall across all IPL seasons?
2. Who took the most wickets across all IPL seasons?
3. Which bowler bowled the most dot balls?

## Key Insights
SK Raina emerged as the most consistent run scorer in the sampled data. 
His volume of runs across seasons points to a player who batted regularly 
in high pressure middle order situations rather than having one standout year.

IK Pathan took the most wickets with 64, closely followed by RP Singh with 63. 
Both players showed consistency across multiple seasons rather than peaking once.

RP Singh was the standout bowler overall. Leading in dot balls with 462 and 
finishing second in wickets confirms he was among IPL's most economical and 
threatening bowlers, consistently suffocating batters while picking up wickets.

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Jupyter Notebook
